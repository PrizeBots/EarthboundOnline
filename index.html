The Engine

199X runs on a high-performance node-datachannel (WebRTC) architecture — real-time action combat that streams straight to your browser. Open a tab and you're in 199X, ready for adventure.


How it works

199X ships code, never game data. You supply your own EarthBound ROM through a file picker in your browser — it's verified, then read entirely on your device to build the sprites, maps, music, and world you see and play. That happens once and is cached, so future visits load instantly. Your ROM is never uploaded. Our server handles only the live multiplayer — relaying player movement, combat, and chat between everyone in the world. It holds no game assets at all.
